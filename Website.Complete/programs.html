Department of Civilian Support - Classes involve: behind the scenes civilian support, (including fundraising tactics to pay for imminent property damage), villain profiling, and media publicity - through the popular medium of movies and comics.

Department of Technological Support - Students are given a comprehensive education about how to best integrate technology into both heroic affairs and civilian support. Many classes will involve case studies of previous superheroes and supervillains and how such technology can be used for the benefit of public order.

Department of Heroic Affairs - A variety of classes are offered; skills taught include villain subjugation, battle field navigation, and a variety of offensive and defensive martial arts techniques. Students are trained to make use of their natural physical strengths.

A Bachelor's of Hero Studies (B.H.S.) is offered in a variety of disciplines, by taking the appropriate courses from each of the above departments. A B.H.S. is offered in Costume Design, Public Media Relations, Economics, Communications and Negotiations, Biotechnology, and more.

Scholarships:

The Tony Stark scholarship - for students who demonstrate ingenuity in supernormal engineering or superhero support technology, apply at starkfoundation.com

The Bruce Wayne scholarship - for students who display an outstanding sense of justice and demonstrate community service and leadership skills. apply at waynefoundation.com
