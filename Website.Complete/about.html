The Hero's SuperUniversity was founded in 1916 by Professor Charles Xavier. His organization, formerly known as the X-men, was created with the purpose of educating and training people who possess supernatural abilities. While the X-Men was originally invite only, the Hero's SuperUniversity is open to public admission. Possession of superhuman abilities is no longer a prerequisite for admission. This year, the Hero's SuperUniversity will celebrate its 100th anniversary since its founding.
